Our Thoughts

Rishabh Narayan Singh

I am pursuing BBA from Invertis University, Bareilly. I am 22 years young! I grew up in a family of people that are very down to earth and find happiness in the little things, so we know where the idea came from. I was an average student, little notorious but always enjoyed keeping the people around me happy. But I still didn't feel that it was enough. Building up an NGO came about in the most unusual way. I had this continuous urge to do my part for society. Casual conversations between friends turned into the day we planned our very first drive. Doing the smallest deed that day gave us immense pleasure. And that was the day we never looked back. Through those experiences I have learned to interact with a diverse group of people, which has increased my ability to relate to others. I understand the needs of the underprivileged and do everything in my power to uplift them and try to give them a better life. The smiles on their faces gave me the joy no other thing could give. I wake up everyday, feeling more grateful and blessed for all the good things I have to adore. Being thankful for another day and having another chance of making someone's day is my current solely objective. “Don’t tell us all the reasons this might not work. Tell us all the ways it could work.”- John Wood (Founder of Room to Read) gives me the daily motivation to get up and get going.

Vibhuti Agarwal

I'm currently pursuing BBA from Invertis University. I'm proud to be a member of Hopes and Dreams, I always wanted to contribute something to our society and Hopes & Dreams gave me that golden opportunity. Hopes & dreams is not only an NGO to me, it's more like a family. I've been working with Hopes & Dreams for more than a year now and it really changed the way I look at life. Being around people with such good hearts and the urge to be able to help is what makes me love this NGO and fills me with an overwhelming feeling of love and happiness.

Sapna Agarwal

I completed my Post Graduation in the year 2020. I speak Hindi and English. I’m proud of being a young member of 'Hopes & Dreams' . My goal is to provide a positive attitude towards our society in the field of growth and development and to learn more and more through new experiences and information . I believe life is too short so stay happy and try to make others even more happier.I feel lucky that I have used this opportunity, to give something back to the society and I will try my best to bring some beautiful changes in everyone's life! This NGO is a collective project with an interdisciplinary team of professionals who is working since August of 2019.
